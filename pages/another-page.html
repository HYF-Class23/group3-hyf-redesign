WHO ARE WE

HackYourFuture's focus on providing coding education to individuals with limited access to education and the labor market, especially refugees, plays a crucial role in empowering them with valuable skills for the digital age.

PROGRAM

We envision a world where education and quality jobs are accessible to all. That's why we've created a free 34-week program tailored for refugees and newcomers, breaking barriers and fostering equal opportunities.
